How to Get Your LinkedIn Cookies
Step 1: Log in to LinkedIn

Make sure you're logged in to LinkedIn.com in the same browser.

Step 2: Open Developer Tools

Right-click anywhere on the LinkedIn page and select "Inspect" or press:

  • Windows: F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I
  • Mac: Cmd+Option+I
Step 3: Access the Application Tab

In the developer tools panel, click on the "Application" tab (you might need to click the >> arrows to find it).

Step 4: Find the Cookies

In the left sidebar, expand "Cookies" and click on "https://www.linkedin.com"

Step 5: Find and Copy the li_at Cookie

In the cookies list, find the cookie named "li_at".

Step 6: Paste Into Cookie String Field

Paste the copied value into the "LinkedIn Cookie String" textarea above.

  • Example cookie string format: li_at=AQEDAVkyDj4ETNv6AAABlfRbcz8AAAGWGGf3P04ALRuXpq_kv5VHWy5Yj13-v6086g811a4thhUegUkgwFslFVbuyipUXVINiBSWxz4yuOH-4x_IEFqePIw4Jb0Q_lVXsvi6EorG9tuhg8dwtMsl3Bdn
Tip: For more reliable authentication, you can also include your other LinkedIn cookies. In the Cookies list, right-click the table header and select "Copy all" to get all cookies in a name=value format, then paste that into the cookie string field.
